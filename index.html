A little about me

Creating since I can remember

I'm Zahraa, a Grade 7 student from Lebanon. I love turning simple materials like cardboard, paper, popsicle sticks, and wood into something beautiful. Every project teaches me something new about shapes, structure, and design. One day, I want to design real buildings that bring people the same joy my small creations bring me.

My Dream

"I want to design buildings that tell stories, spaces where people feel at home, and structures that stand as works of art."

Every mosque I shape from cardboard, every Victorian house I construct from scraps, every amusement park I build piece by piece is practice for the real thing. Architecture is where art meets engineering, and I can't wait to bring my designs to life at full scale.
