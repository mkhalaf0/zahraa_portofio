A little about me

Creating since I can remember

I'm Zahraa, a Grade 7 student from Lebanon. I love turning simple materials like cardboard, paper, popsicle sticks, and clay into something beautiful. Every project teaches me something new about shapes, structure, and design. One day, I want to design real buildings that bring people the same joy my small creations bring me.
